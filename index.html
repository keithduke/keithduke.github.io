About

Hello, my name is Keith Duke.

I am a web developer with over 13 years of experience working in the online media industry. For a decade I served as a front-end engineer for CBS Interactive ( now Paramount ), as both a Lead and Senior on numerous brands. Working with various delivery technologies such as PHP and frameworks like Laravel, CodeIgniter, Zend, and Python with Django; Javascript and Node with Vue/Nuxt and React; CSS with Sass, BEM, CSS Modules, and major frameworks like bootstrap.

I completed my Bachelor of Arts in Web Design & New Media at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco. I previously completed coursework in Computer Science at Middle Tennessee State University. I have a background in craft having worked with wood, glass, book, paper and print arts. I fancy myself a writer, coding is an act of writing to two audiences.

Read more of my writing at Here Lately, a weblog I am building with no frameworks or external build tools, just html, css and browser based javascript.

Reach out to me on Linkedin, Github or Mastodon.
